Specialists in production and postproduction in various High Definition formats (2K, 4K and beyond), Infinity Vision Production (VIP) is one of the oldest, most reputed production houses in the UAE, with resources to rival any large production firm.

Serving a host of clients across a spectrum of businesses in the commercial and industrial sectors, we are the one-stop solutions vendor for post-production in video and film production in Dubai Media City.

Over the years, our professional work ethics has given us the accountability and experience in the pan-Arab market.
